Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

DiligenceOS Global Pvt. Ltd. ("DiligenceOS", "we", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of the businesses and individuals we work with. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of this website:

  • Contact details - such as your name, email address, company, and phone number when you request a consultation or get in touch.
  • Engagement information - financial and accounting records you share with us in the course of a service engagement, handled under a separate engagement agreement.
  • Usage data - basic, aggregated information about how visitors use this site, such as pages viewed and general location.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and schedule consultations.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our bookkeeping, accounting, payroll, tax, and advisory services.
  • To meet our legal, regulatory, and professional obligations.
  • To communicate updates relevant to a service you have requested.

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies

This website uses a minimal set of cookies and local browser storage to remember your preferences (for example, your light or dark theme choice). We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Managing cookies: You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Because we rely only on essential preference storage, disabling it will not break the site - you may simply need to re-set your preferences on your next visit.

How We Protect Your Information

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard the information entrusted to us, and we restrict access to client data to the team members who need it to deliver a service. Engagement data is handled in line with the confidentiality terms set out in your service agreement.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you, and you may ask us to stop sending you communications. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
